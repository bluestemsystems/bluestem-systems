Who it's for

Bluestem works with small and online service businesses and solopreneurs who have grown past what they can manage alone. If any of these sound familiar, you're in the right place.

  • You're a coach, consultant, course creator, or small business owner running your business through a stack of tools that never quite work together
  • You've got clients, revenue, and momentum — but your backend is chaos and you know it
  • Your books are somewhere between "a little behind" and "I haven't looked since Q1"
  • You have processes, but they live in your head and nowhere else
  • You've hired a VA or a small team and realized you have no documentation to hand them
  • You want to grow, but the operations layer of your business can't keep up

About

Bluestem is a small team operation based in Central Texas, working remotely with online businesses across the country. We specialize in the backend layer — the systems, tools, processes, and books that keep a business running but rarely get the attention they need.

Our background is in office operations and process building for technology companies, and we have multiple staff certified with QuickBooks ProAdvisor. We work best with business owners who are excellent at what they do and ready to stop being their own operations department.
